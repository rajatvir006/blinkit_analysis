Why Forecasting Matters

Retail sales fluctuate due to seasonality, product demand, outlet size, and consumer trends. Forecasting helps businesses plan ahead for inventory, pricing, and supply-chain optimization.

How This Project Helps

This model predicts monthly revenue ($) using product and outlet parameters. It enables data-backed decisions by blending historical data with statistical learning for efficient retail management.

Model Overview

  • Algorithm: Random Forest Regressor
  • Target: Monthly Revenue ($)
  • Performance: R² ≈ 0.54

Input Features

  • Item: Weight, Visibility, Type, Fat Content
  • Outlet: Type, Size, Location, Age

Data & Training

Historical Blinkit retail data (2011–2022) was cleaned, preprocessed, and one-hot encoded for accurate predictions aligned with real-world outlet performance.

Key Insights

  • Medium-sized outlets show the strongest consistency in revenue.
  • Regular-fat items contribute more to total sales than low-fat products.
  • High visibility correlates with higher item performance.

Use Cases

  • Compare store performance by type and region.
  • Identify underperforming outlets using dashboards.
  • Plan future sales and marketing strategies efficiently.
